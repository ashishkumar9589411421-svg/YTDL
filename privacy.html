Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

We believe in minimalism. In.dl collects only the bare minimum required:

  • IP Addresses: Used strictly for rate limiting (5 downloads/day for guests) and abuse prevention.
  • User Accounts: If you register, we store your username and a hashed (encrypted) version of your password.
  • Payment Proofs: Screenshots uploaded for plan upgrades are stored securely for verification.

2. File Retention

We do not store your files permanently.

  • When you request a download, our server temporarily processes the Instagram media.
  • Automatic Deletion: All downloaded files are automatically wiped from our servers within 1 hour.
  • We do not keep logs of what specific content you download.

3. Cookies

We use a single authentication token (JWT) stored in your browser to keep you logged in. No third-party tracking pixels.

4. Contact

If you have privacy concerns or wish to request data deletion, please contact the admin via the support email.
